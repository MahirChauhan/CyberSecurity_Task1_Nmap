
 What is an open port?	A network port that accepts incoming connections.
 How does Nmap perform TCP SYN scan?	It sends SYN packets and checks for SYN-ACK responses without completing the handshake.
 Risks of open ports?	Unauthorized access, malware, or data breaches.
 TCP vs UDP scan?	TCP is connection-based, UDP is connectionless.
 How to secure open ports?	Close unused ports, use firewalls, apply access control.
 Role of firewall?	Blocks or allows traffic on specific ports.
 Why attackers perform port scans?	To find vulnerable services to exploit.
